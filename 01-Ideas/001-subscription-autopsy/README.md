# 1. Subscription Autopsy

> AI scans bank statements to find and cancel forgotten subscriptions

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | Personal Finance |
| **Target Audience** | Working Class |
| **Monetization** | Freemium |
| **Recession Resistance** | High |
| **Build Effort** | Medium - 8-12 weeks MVP |

## Problem

Americans waste $133/month on forgotten subscriptions. Free trials auto-convert. People don't know what they're paying for.

## Solution

Connect via Plaid, scan transactions, identify recurring charges, show lifetime cost of each, provide one-tap cancel links with step-by-step guides for stubborn services.

## Market Size

$2B subscription management market

## Existing Competitors

Truebill/Rocket Money, Trim

## Competitive Moat

AI pattern detection for non-obvious subscriptions (annual charges, price-creeping services)
