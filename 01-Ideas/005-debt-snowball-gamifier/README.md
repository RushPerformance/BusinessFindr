# 5. Debt Snowball Gamifier

> Turns debt payoff into a game with streaks, milestones, and community

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | Personal Finance |
| **Target Audience** | Working Class |
| **Monetization** | Freemium |
| **Recession Resistance** | High |
| **Build Effort** | Low - 6-8 weeks MVP |

## Problem

Americans hold $17T in debt. Debt payoff is psychologically crushing — 60% of people abandon their payoff plans within 3 months.

## Solution

Connect accounts, visualize debt as monsters/levels to defeat, daily streaks for staying on budget, community challenges, milestone celebrations, and progress leaderboards.

## Market Size

$1.5B debt management market

## Existing Competitors

Undebt.it, Tally, Debt Payoff Planner

## Competitive Moat

Gamification + community — competitors are spreadsheets with apps
