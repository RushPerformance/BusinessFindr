# 6. Micro-Investment Roundup for Kids

> Parents' card roundups go into custodial investment accounts with kid education

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | Personal Finance |
| **Target Audience** | Mass Market |
| **Monetization** | Subscription |
| **Recession Resistance** | Medium |
| **Build Effort** | Medium - 12 weeks MVP |

## Problem

Financial literacy is abysmal — only 22% of teens understand basic investing. Parents want to teach kids about money but don't know how.

## Solution

Link parent cards, round up purchases, invest in custodial account. Kids see portfolio grow, get age-appropriate lessons on what each company does.

## Market Size

$3.1B kids fintech market

## Existing Competitors

Greenlight, Acorns Early Invest

## Competitive Moat

Education-first approach — kids learn what they own, not just that numbers go up
