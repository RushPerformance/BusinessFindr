# 7. Bill Negotiation Bot

> AI calls providers to negotiate lower rates on your bills

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | Personal Finance |
| **Target Audience** | Working Class |
| **Monetization** | Commission |
| **Recession Resistance** | High |
| **Build Effort** | High - 16+ weeks MVP |

## Problem

Average household overpays $900/year on cable, internet, insurance, and phone bills. Negotiating is tedious and time-consuming.

## Solution

AI agent calls providers, navigates phone trees, negotiates using proven scripts, reports back with savings. Takes 25-30% of first year savings as fee.

## Market Size

$900/household addressable × 130M households

## Existing Competitors

Billshark, Trim

## Competitive Moat

Fully automated AI calling — competitors use human negotiators (expensive, slow)
