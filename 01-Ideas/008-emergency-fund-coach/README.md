# 8. Emergency Fund Coach

> Behavioral nudges and micro-savings to build a 3-month emergency buffer

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | Personal Finance |
| **Target Audience** | Working Class |
| **Monetization** | Freemium |
| **Recession Resistance** | High |
| **Build Effort** | Low - 6 weeks MVP |

## Problem

56% of Americans can't cover a $1,000 emergency. Saving feels impossible on tight budgets, and traditional advice ('save 20%') is unrealistic.

## Solution

Micro-savings triggers: round-ups, payday bumps, 'skip the latte' opt-ins. Goal visualization showing days of runway. Celebration milestones at $500, $1K, $2K.

## Market Size

$4.5B personal savings market

## Existing Competitors

Digit, Qapital, Chime savings

## Competitive Moat

Psychological approach — tracks 'days of freedom' not just dollars, recession-specific features
