# 9. Couples Money Mediator

> Shared finance dashboard that resolves money fights before they start

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | Personal Finance |
| **Target Audience** | Mass Market |
| **Monetization** | Subscription |
| **Recession Resistance** | Medium |
| **Build Effort** | Medium - 10 weeks MVP |

## Problem

Money is the #1 cause of relationship stress and divorce. Couples avoid financial conversations until crisis hits.

## Solution

Joint dashboard showing shared goals, individual spending, and upcoming obligations. Disagreement flags with suggested compromises. Monthly 'money date' prompts.

## Market Size

$2B couples/family finance market

## Existing Competitors

Honeydue, Goodbudget

## Competitive Moat

Conflict resolution angle — not just tracking, actively mediating disagreements
