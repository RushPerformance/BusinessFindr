# 10. Price Drop Refund Hunter

> Auto-files price adjustment claims when items you bought drop in price

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | Personal Finance |
| **Target Audience** | Mass Market |
| **Monetization** | Commission |
| **Recession Resistance** | Medium |
| **Build Effort** | Medium - 10 weeks MVP |

## Problem

Retailers quietly drop prices days after purchase. Most people never check, losing $50-200/year in potential refunds.

## Solution

Connect email for receipt scanning, monitor prices on purchased items, auto-file price adjustment claims or credit card price protection claims.

## Market Size

$500M price protection market

## Existing Competitors

Paribus (acquired by Capital One), Earny

## Competitive Moat

Independent — not locked into one credit card ecosystem
