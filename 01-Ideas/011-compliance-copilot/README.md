# 11. Compliance Copilot

> AI-powered compliance checklist engine that tells small businesses exactly what regulations apply to them

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | Compliance / B2B SaaS |
| **Target Audience** | SMBs with 5-50 employees (restaurants, clinics, contractors, retail) |
| **Monetization** | Subscription ($49-149/mo) |
| **Recession Resistance** | High - regulations don't pause in downturns; fines increase enforcement pressure |
| **Build Effort** | Medium - 10-14 weeks MVP |

## Problem

Small businesses face an average of 5,000+ federal, state, and local regulations. 68% of SMBs report they've been fined or penalized for a compliance issue they didn't know existed (NFIB 2024 survey). The average compliance fine for a small business is $12,000. Most can't afford a compliance officer ($75K+ salary) and rely on Google searches and hope.

**Reddit signal**: r/smallbusiness threads consistently show owners blindsided by OSHA, ADA, state labor law, and food safety violations. Top recurring complaint: "I didn't even know that was a rule."

## Solution

User answers 10 questions about their business (industry, state, employee count, revenue). The system generates a personalized compliance checklist with deadlines, auto-reminders, and plain-English explanations. AI monitors regulatory changes and pushes alerts.

## Market Size

$12.3B global regulatory compliance market (Grand View Research); SMB segment ~$2.1B and growing 14% YoY.

## Revenue Potential

5,000 SMBs at $99/mo = $5.9M ARR. Low churn — businesses can't afford to cancel compliance tools.

## Existing Competitors

ComplyAdvantage (enterprise-focused), Compliancy Group (HIPAA-only), manual consulting firms. No dominant horizontal SMB compliance tool exists.

## Competitive Moat

Regulation database + AI personalization engine. Network effects as more businesses in a jurisdiction provide data. Switching costs increase as compliance history accumulates.
