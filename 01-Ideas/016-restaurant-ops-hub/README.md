# 16. Restaurant Ops Hub

> Unified back-of-house operations for independent restaurants: food cost tracking, waste logging, and vendor management

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | SMB Tools / B2B SaaS |
| **Target Audience** | Independent restaurants and small restaurant groups (1-5 locations) |
| **Monetization** | Subscription ($79-149/mo per location) |
| **Recession Resistance** | Medium - restaurants are cyclical, but food cost management becomes more critical during downturns when margins compress from 5% to 2-3% |
| **Build Effort** | Medium - 12-14 weeks MVP |

## Problem

60% of restaurants fail within the first year, 80% within five years (National Restaurant Association). Average restaurant profit margins are 3-5%. Food costs represent 28-35% of revenue, and the average restaurant wastes 4-10% of purchased food. Most independent restaurants track food costs on paper or not at all.

**Reddit signal**: r/restaurateur and r/KitchenConfidential are filled with complaints about Toast, Square for Restaurants, and MarketMan. Top pain points: "Toast nickel-and-dimes us on every feature," "I just want to know my food cost percentage without a finance degree," "inventory counting takes my manager 6 hours a week."

## Solution

Photo-based invoice scanning for automatic food cost tracking. Waste log with categorization (spoilage, overproduction, prep error). Recipe costing calculator. Vendor price comparison dashboard. Weekly P&L snapshot via text/email. Integration with Toast, Square, Clover POS.

## Market Size

$1M+ independent restaurants in the US. Restaurant management software market: $6.9B (Grand View Research). Back-of-house segment underserved vs. POS-heavy front-of-house.

## Revenue Potential

3,000 restaurants at $99/mo = $3.6M ARR.

## Existing Competitors

MarketMan, BlueCart, xtraCHEF (acquired by Toast), Restaurant365. All either enterprise-priced ($300+/mo) or absorbed into POS ecosystems with lock-in.

## Competitive Moat

POS-agnostic design (works with Toast, Square, Clover, or none). Photo invoice scanning removes the biggest adoption friction. Purpose-built for independents, not chains.
