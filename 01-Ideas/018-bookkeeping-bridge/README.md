# 18. Bookkeeping Bridge

> AI categorizes bank transactions and generates monthly books — the gap between "doing nothing" and "hiring a bookkeeper"

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | SMB Tools / B2B SaaS |
| **Target Audience** | Solo businesses and micro-businesses doing $50K-$500K revenue who can't justify a bookkeeper |
| **Monetization** | Subscription ($29-59/mo) |
| **Recession Resistance** | High - tax obligations and financial tracking are non-negotiable; recessions increase the need to watch every dollar |
| **Build Effort** | Medium - 10-12 weeks MVP |

## Problem

50% of small business owners do their own bookkeeping despite having no training (QuickBooks survey). The average small business bookkeeping error rate is 40% when self-managed. A bookkeeper costs $300-800/mo. QuickBooks and Xero require 3+ hours/week of transaction categorization that most owners skip, leading to year-end tax scrambles.

**Reddit signal**: r/smallbusiness bookkeeping threads are some of the most upvoted. Pain point: "I connected QuickBooks to my bank but I still have 1,200 uncategorized transactions." Owners want a system that just works without them touching it.

## Solution

Connect bank accounts via Plaid. AI auto-categorizes 90%+ of transactions using business-type-specific rules. Owner confirms or corrects via swipe interface (Tinder for transactions). Monthly financial snapshot delivered by email. Tax-ready reports generated automatically. Escalates edge cases to human bookkeepers in marketplace (revenue share).

## Market Size

$4.8B SMB accounting software market (Statista). The "bookkeeping gap" (too small for a bookkeeper, too complex for nothing) serves ~8M US businesses.

## Revenue Potential

10,000 businesses at $39/mo average = $4.7M ARR. Human bookkeeper marketplace adds commission revenue.

## Existing Competitors

QuickBooks, Xero, Bench (human bookkeepers, $299+/mo), Pilot ($400+/mo). QBO and Xero are powerful but require owner engagement. Bench and Pilot are expensive.

## Competitive Moat

AI categorization accuracy improves with data. Swipe UX makes the daily interaction 30 seconds instead of 30 minutes. Price point fills a gap that QBO (too complex) and Bench (too expensive) miss.
