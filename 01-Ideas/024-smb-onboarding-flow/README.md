# 24. SMB Onboarding Flow

> Automated client onboarding workflows for service businesses — collect documents, signatures, and payments in one link

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | SMB Tools / B2B SaaS |
| **Target Audience** | Accountants, lawyers, financial advisors, agencies, and consultants who onboard 5-50 new clients/month |
| **Monetization** | Subscription ($29-79/mo based on client volume) |
| **Recession Resistance** | Medium - new client volume drops in recessions, but operational efficiency becomes more important with fewer staff |
| **Build Effort** | Low - 6-8 weeks MVP |

## Problem

Professional service firms spend 3-8 hours onboarding each new client (emails back and forth for documents, contracts, intake forms, payment setup). 23% of client relationships start with a bad onboarding experience that leads to early churn (ClientPoint data). The current workflow: email a contract, wait, chase, get a signed PDF, email an intake form, wait, chase, manually enter data, set up billing.

**Reddit signal**: r/Accounting, r/LawFirm, and r/Entrepreneur threads repeatedly cite onboarding friction. "I lose 10% of signed deals because the onboarding process takes too long and clients ghost." Accountants are especially vocal about tax season onboarding chaos.

## Solution

Create a branded onboarding portal with drag-and-drop steps: contract signing, intake form, document upload, payment method collection, welcome video. Client gets one link. Provider gets a dashboard tracking completion. Auto-reminders for incomplete steps.

## Market Size

$1.2B client onboarding software market (Allied Market Research). Professional services subset: ~$500M.

## Revenue Potential

5,000 firms at $49/mo average = $2.9M ARR.

## Existing Competitors

Dubsado, HoneyBook (creative-focused), Practice (accounting-focused), Content Snare (document collection only). No horizontal SMB onboarding tool that does everything in one flow.

## Competitive Moat

Onboarding templates by industry create a content moat. Client completion data improves the product (AI optimizes step order and reminder timing). Once integrated into a firm's workflow, switching costs are high.
