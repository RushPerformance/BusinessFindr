# 25. Review Responder

> AI drafts personalized responses to Google, Yelp, and Facebook reviews in your brand voice

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | SMB Tools / B2B SaaS |
| **Target Audience** | Local businesses (restaurants, salons, clinics, auto shops) managing online reputation across 2-5 platforms |
| **Monetization** | Subscription ($19-49/mo per location) |
| **Recession Resistance** | High - online reputation directly impacts revenue; 93% of consumers say online reviews influence purchasing decisions (BrightLocal) |
| **Build Effort** | Low - 4-6 weeks MVP |

## Problem

76% of consumers expect businesses to respond to reviews (BrightLocal 2024). But 53% of customers expect a response within 7 days, and the average SMB takes 3+ weeks. Responding to reviews takes 15-30 minutes each for thoughtful replies. A one-star improvement on Yelp leads to a 5-9% increase in revenue (Harvard Business School study). Most SMB owners know this but don't have the time.

**Reddit signal**: r/smallbusiness review management threads are consistently popular. "I have 47 unanswered Google reviews and I know it's hurting me but I just can't find the time." Multi-location owners especially struggling.

## Solution

Connect Google Business, Yelp, Facebook, TripAdvisor accounts. AI drafts responses matching the business's tone (professional, casual, warm). Owner approves with one tap or edits. Negative review escalation alerts. Review trend analytics. Review request campaigns to satisfied customers.

## Market Size

$4.1B reputation management software market (Grand View Research). SMB/local business segment: ~$1.2B.

## Revenue Potential

15,000 locations at $29/mo average = $5.2M ARR. One of the highest-volume opportunities on this list.

## Existing Competitors

Podium ($399+/mo), Birdeye ($299+/mo), Reputation.com (enterprise). These tools do 50 things — review response is a feature, not the focus. Way overpriced for a single-location business.

## Competitive Moat

AI response quality improves with brand voice training data. Simple, single-purpose tool vs. bloated reputation suites. Price point ($29/mo vs $399/mo) unlocks a market 10x larger than what incumbents serve.
