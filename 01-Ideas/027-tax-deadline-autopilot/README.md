# 27. Tax Deadline Autopilot

> Personalized tax deadline calendar for small businesses with automated filing reminders and prep checklists

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | Compliance / SMB Tools |
| **Target Audience** | Small business owners, freelancers, and their accountants dealing with federal, state, and local tax deadlines |
| **Monetization** | Subscription ($15-39/mo for business owners; $49-99/mo for accountants managing multiple clients) |
| **Recession Resistance** | High - tax obligations don't decrease during recessions; penalties for late filing increase urgency |
| **Build Effort** | Low - 6-8 weeks MVP |

## Problem

Small businesses face 15-30+ tax deadlines per year across income tax, payroll tax, sales tax, and local taxes. IRS penalty revenue exceeded $7B in 2024 — much of it from small businesses filing or paying late. The average late filing penalty is $435/month. 60% of small businesses have paid a penalty for a missed tax deadline at some point (NSBA survey).

**Reddit signal**: r/tax and r/smallbusiness regularly feature panic posts about missed estimated tax payments, forgotten state filings, and surprise penalties. "I didn't know [state] had a separate LLC annual fee with its own deadline." Accountants in r/Accounting want a tool to proactively manage deadlines across 50+ clients.

## Solution

Input business type, entity structure, state(s), and whether you have employees. System generates a personalized annual tax calendar. Auto-reminders via text/email at configurable intervals. Prep checklists for each deadline (documents needed, forms, where to file). Integration with calendar apps. Accountant dashboard for multi-client management.

## Market Size

$2.3B tax compliance software market for SMBs (IBISWorld). Deadline management is a $300-500M subsegment.

## Revenue Potential

12,000 users at $25/mo average = $3.6M ARR. Accountant tier drives higher ARPU and retention.

## Existing Competitors

Tax software (TurboTax, H&R Block) focuses on filing, not deadline management. Canopy (for accountants, $50+/user/mo). No standalone tax deadline tool for SMB owners themselves.

## Competitive Moat

Jurisdiction-specific deadline database is a significant data asset. Accountant network creates distribution. Once embedded in a business's workflow, switching is risky (missing a deadline has real financial consequences).
