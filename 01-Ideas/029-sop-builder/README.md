# 29. SOP Builder

> Create, manage, and train employees on standard operating procedures with AI-generated documentation

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | B2B SaaS / SMB Tools |
| **Target Audience** | SMBs with 5-50 employees in operations-heavy industries (restaurants, manufacturing, clinics, salons, franchises) |
| **Monetization** | Subscription ($29-79/mo based on employee count) |
| **Recession Resistance** | Medium - process documentation is often deprioritized during downturns, but businesses that invest in SOPs reduce training costs by 30-50% |
| **Build Effort** | Low - 6-8 weeks MVP |

## Problem

Businesses without documented SOPs lose 20-30% productivity during employee turnover (Process Street data). Average employee onboarding takes 60-90 days; SOPs reduce this by 40%. 80% of small business knowledge lives in the owner's head — the "hit by a bus" problem. Creating SOPs from scratch is tedious; most attempts end with a forgotten Google Doc.

**Reddit signal**: r/smallbusiness and r/Entrepreneur threads about "how to document processes" are always high-engagement. McDonald's-style operations manuals are aspirational for small business owners. "I need to clone myself" is the recurring theme. Indie Hackers has multiple success stories around process/knowledge management tools.

## Solution

Record a screen (Loom-like) or describe a process verbally. AI generates step-by-step SOP with screenshots, checklists, and decision trees. Employees access SOPs via mobile. Track who's read/completed each SOP. Version control for updates. Quiz-based training verification.

## Market Size

$1.4B knowledge management software market for SMBs (Verified Market Research). SOP-specific tools: ~$350M.

## Revenue Potential

5,000 businesses at $49/mo average = $2.9M ARR.

## Existing Competitors

Trainual ($99+/mo), Process Street ($25+/user/mo = expensive for teams), SweetProcess, Whale. Trainual is the closest but priced for 20+ employee companies ($99/mo minimum).

## Competitive Moat

AI-generated SOPs from video/audio recordings dramatically reduce creation friction. Process library across industries becomes a content asset. Training verification creates compliance value that drives retention.
