# 31. Appointment No-Show Reducer

> AI-optimized reminder sequences and deposit collection to cut no-shows by 60%+

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | SMB Tools / B2B SaaS |
| **Target Audience** | Service businesses with appointments: salons, clinics, dentists, auto shops, consultants, tattoo shops |
| **Monetization** | Subscription ($19-49/mo per location) |
| **Recession Resistance** | High - no-shows are more costly during downturns when every appointment matters; ROI is immediately measurable |
| **Build Effort** | Low - 4-6 weeks MVP |

## Problem

The average no-show rate across service industries is 20-30%. For medical practices, it's 23% (SCI Solutions). Each no-show costs a salon $50-150, a medical practice $200+, and a consultant $300-1,000 in lost revenue. A salon with 30 appointments/day losing 20% to no-shows forfeits $90K-270K annually. Current calendar tools send one reminder; research shows multi-channel, multi-touch sequences reduce no-shows by 60-80%.

**Reddit signal**: r/salons, r/dentistry, r/smallbusiness: "No-shows are killing my business." Threads about charging deposits generate heated debate — businesses want to collect deposits but fear losing customers. The real problem is confirmation friction.

## Solution

Multi-channel confirmation sequences (SMS, email, push) with optimized timing. Easy 1-tap confirm/reschedule in the message. Configurable deposit collection for high-value appointments. No-show prediction based on booking patterns (first-time client, weekend appointment, etc.). Waitlist auto-fill for cancellations.

## Market Size

$546M appointment reminder and scheduling notification market (within $4.4B appointment scheduling software market).

## Revenue Potential

10,000 locations at $29/mo = $3.5M ARR. Transaction fees on deposits add revenue.

## Existing Competitors

Most calendar/scheduling tools (Calendly, Acuity, Square Appointments) send basic reminders. No tool focuses specifically on no-show reduction with multi-channel optimization and prediction. GoReminders ($25+/mo) is closest but lacks prediction and deposits.

## Competitive Moat

Behavioral data on what reminder sequences work best by industry, appointment type, and client history. Prediction model improves with scale. Measurable ROI makes churn low — if the tool saves you $500/mo, you won't cancel a $29/mo subscription.
