# 32. Lease & Contract Tracker

> Never miss a renewal deadline, auto-renewal trap, or unfavorable contract term again

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | SMB Tools / B2B SaaS |
| **Target Audience** | SMBs managing 10-100+ vendor contracts, leases, and service agreements |
| **Monetization** | Subscription ($29-69/mo based on contract volume) |
| **Recession Resistance** | High - contract management becomes critical during downturns when businesses need to renegotiate or exit unfavorable agreements |
| **Build Effort** | Low - 6-8 weeks MVP |

## Problem

Small businesses manage an average of 20-40 active contracts (leases, SaaS subscriptions, vendor agreements, insurance policies). 60% of businesses have missed a contract renewal or cancellation window (World Commerce & Contracting). Auto-renewals cost businesses an average of $4,800/year in unwanted contract extensions. Reading contract fine print is time-consuming — critical terms like auto-renewal clauses, rate escalation provisions, and exclusivity restrictions are buried.

**Reddit signal**: r/smallbusiness and r/Entrepreneur feature regular frustrations about auto-renewing contracts, missed cancellation windows, and surprise rate increases. "Our copier lease auto-renewed for 3 years because we missed the 90-day cancellation window. $18,000 locked in."

## Solution

Upload or email-forward contracts. AI extracts key terms: renewal dates, cancellation windows, rate escalation clauses, liability limits. Centralized dashboard with timeline view. Automated alerts at configurable intervals before deadlines. Side-by-side contract comparison. Notes and negotiation history per contract.

## Market Size

$2.1B contract lifecycle management market (Grand View Research). SMB segment: ~$450M. Massively underserved — most CLM tools target enterprise.

## Revenue Potential

6,000 businesses at $49/mo = $3.5M ARR.

## Existing Competitors

DocuSign CLM ($30+/user/mo, enterprise-focused), Ironclad (enterprise), ContractWorks ($600+/mo). No affordable, simple contract tracker for SMBs. Most small businesses use spreadsheets or nothing.

## Competitive Moat

AI term extraction improves with more contracts. Contract history becomes a critical business record that's hard to migrate. Renewal alerts create measurable savings that justify the subscription. True whitespace opportunity at the SMB level.
