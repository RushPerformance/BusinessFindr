# 33. Tip & Commission Splitter

> Automate tip pooling, commission calculations, and payroll integration for service businesses

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | SMB Tools / B2B SaaS |
| **Target Audience** | Restaurants, salons, spas, bars, hotels, and any business with tipped or commission-based employees |
| **Monetization** | Per-employee pricing ($3-6/employee/mo) or flat $29-59/mo |
| **Recession Resistance** | High - tip distribution is legally required and operationally mandatory; DOL enforcement of tip pooling rules is increasing |
| **Build Effort** | Low - 6-8 weeks MVP |

## Problem

Tip pooling regulations vary by state and changed significantly under the FLSA 2024 updates. 58% of restaurant owners manually calculate tip distribution in spreadsheets or on paper (Toast survey). Errors in tip allocation can trigger DOL audits and employee lawsuits — average settlement: $25,000. For commission-based businesses (salons, real estate teams), calculating tiered commissions with bonuses and draws is a weekly headache that takes managers 2-4 hours.

**Reddit signal**: r/restaurateur, r/bartenders, r/salons threads about tip pool fairness, calculation errors, and legal compliance are extremely common. "I spend every Monday morning doing tip math for 30 employees." Multiple threads about lawsuits from incorrect tip pooling.

## Solution

Configure tip pool rules (percentage splits, position-based, hours-worked weighting). POS integration (Toast, Square, Clover) to auto-import tip data. One-click tip distribution calculation. Commission calculator with tiered structures, bonuses, and draws. Export to payroll (Gusto, ADP, Paychex). Compliance documentation and audit trail.

## Market Size

Niche within the $8.5B payroll services market. Direct addressable: ~$200M for tip/commission management specifically. 660,000+ restaurants and 1M+ salons in the US.

## Revenue Potential

5,000 businesses x 15 employees x $4/mo = $3.6M ARR.

## Existing Competitors

7shifts (has basic tip pooling), Kickfin (tip distribution only, $5/employee/mo), TipHaus. No tool combines tip pooling + commission calculation + multi-POS integration at an affordable price.

## Competitive Moat

POS integrations create switching costs. State-specific compliance rules engine. Payroll integration makes the tool part of the weekly operational workflow — very sticky.
