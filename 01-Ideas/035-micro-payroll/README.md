# 35. Micro Payroll

> Dead-simple payroll for businesses with 1-10 employees — no accountant required

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | SMB Tools / B2B SaaS |
| **Target Audience** | Micro-businesses with 1-10 employees (cafes, shops, trades, services) |
| **Monetization** | Base + per-employee ($20/mo + $4/employee/mo) |
| **Recession Resistance** | High - payroll is legally mandatory; businesses must pay employees and file payroll taxes regardless of economic conditions |
| **Build Effort** | High - 14-18 weeks MVP (payroll tax calculations and filing require precision and regulatory compliance) |

## Problem

40% of small businesses incur an average of $845/year in payroll penalties (IRS data). The average small business spends 5 hours/month on payroll administration. Gusto starts at $40/mo + $6/employee — a 5-person business pays $70/mo. For a 2-person shop, that's disproportionately expensive. Many micro-businesses resort to paying employees via Venmo/Zelle and skip payroll taxes, creating massive legal liability.

**Reddit signal**: r/smallbusiness payroll threads reveal two camps: (1) those using Gusto/ADP who find it expensive for their size, and (2) those doing payroll "informally" who are terrified of IRS consequences. "Is there a payroll service that doesn't cost more than one of my employees earns in a day?"

## Solution

Guided setup: business info, employee info, pay rates. Automatic tax calculations (federal, state, local). Direct deposit via ACH. Tax form generation and filing (941, 940, W-2, state equivalents). Year-end processing. Simple time tracking integration. No upsells.

## Market Size

$6.2B small business payroll market (IBISWorld). Micro-business segment (1-10 employees): ~$1.8B.

## Revenue Potential

8,000 businesses x avg 4 employees x ($20 + $16)/mo = $2.3M ARR.

## Existing Competitors

Gusto ($40+/mo base), ADP Run ($59+/mo base), Paychex ($39+/mo base), Wave Payroll ($20+/mo — closest on price but limited state tax filing). Square Payroll ($35/mo) is competitive but tied to Square ecosystem.

## Competitive Moat

Price leadership in a commodity market. Radical simplicity for non-technical owners. State tax filing coverage (Wave only covers 14 states; full 50-state coverage is a real differentiator). Payroll is the stickiest software a business uses — switching payroll providers is extremely rare.
