# 38. Quote-to-Cash Pipeline

> From quote request to paid invoice in one flow — built for trades and home services

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | SMB Tools / B2B SaaS |
| **Target Audience** | Contractors, landscapers, painters, roofers, and home service businesses giving 10-100+ quotes/month |
| **Monetization** | Subscription ($29-69/mo) |
| **Recession Resistance** | High - home repair/maintenance is essential; businesses that quote faster win more jobs, which is especially critical during downturns |
| **Build Effort** | Medium - 8-10 weeks MVP |

## Problem

Trades businesses lose 30-40% of potential revenue to slow quoting (Jobber research). The average contractor takes 3-7 days to send a quote after a site visit. 50% of homeowners hire the first contractor who provides a quote (HomeAdvisor data). Most contractors create quotes in Word/Excel, email them as PDFs, then manually create invoices later for won jobs. There's no connection between the quote, the approval, the job schedule, and the invoice.

**Reddit signal**: r/Construction, r/landscaping, r/HomeImprovement (from the contractor perspective) consistently cite quoting speed as a competitive advantage. "I started sending quotes from my truck on the same day and my close rate went from 25% to 60%." But no affordable tool lets them do quote-to-invoice in one flow.

## Solution

Mobile-first quote creation from saved line items and labor rates. Photo attachment from the job site. Client approval via text link (tap to approve). Approved quote auto-converts to scheduled job. Job completion triggers invoice generation. Payment collection via link (Stripe). Simple P&L tracking per job.

## Market Size

$3.1B quoting and estimating software market (MarketsandMarkets). Home services segment: ~$800M.

## Revenue Potential

5,000 businesses at $49/mo = $2.9M ARR. Payment processing fees add 15-20%.

## Existing Competitors

Jobber ($49+/mo), ServiceFusion ($126+/mo), Estimate Rocket, Joist (acquired by GoDaddy). Most are becoming full FSM platforms with feature bloat. Price creep is constant. No tool focuses purely on the quote-to-cash flow with extreme simplicity.

## Competitive Moat

Speed — if the product can get a quote sent in 5 minutes from the truck, it sells itself via word of mouth. Line item pricing database improves with usage (suggest prices based on regional data). Transaction fees on payments create revenue beyond subscriptions.
