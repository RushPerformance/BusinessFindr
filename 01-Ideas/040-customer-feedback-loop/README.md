# 40. Customer Feedback Loop

> Collect, analyze, and act on customer feedback from multiple channels — NPS, surveys, reviews, and support tickets in one view

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Category** | B2B SaaS / SMB Tools |
| **Target Audience** | SaaS companies, e-commerce stores, and service businesses with 100-10,000 customers |
| **Monetization** | Subscription ($29-79/mo based on response volume) |
| **Recession Resistance** | Medium - customer retention becomes critical during recessions, making feedback tools more valuable; but some businesses cut "non-essential" tools |
| **Build Effort** | Medium - 8-10 weeks MVP |

## Problem

Customer feedback is scattered across review sites, support tickets, social media comments, NPS surveys, and direct emails. 80% of companies believe they deliver excellent customer experience, but only 8% of customers agree (Bain & Company). Small businesses lack the resources for dedicated customer success teams. Enterprise tools (Qualtrics, Medallia) cost $10,000+/year.

**Reddit signal**: r/SaaS and r/Entrepreneur threads show founders struggling to consolidate feedback. "I get feature requests in Intercom, complaints on Twitter, reviews on G2, and NPS responses in Typeform. I have no single view of what customers actually want." Indie Hackers founders cite "building what customers ask for" as their #1 success factor.

## Solution

Unified inbox pulling from email, support tools (Intercom, Zendesk), review sites, social media, and in-app surveys. AI categorizes feedback by theme (pricing, UX, features, bugs, praise). Sentiment tracking over time. Feature request voting board (public or private). Auto-tagging and trend detection. Monthly digest reports.

## Market Size

$3.8B customer experience management market for SMBs (Grand View Research). Feedback aggregation subset: ~$800M.

## Revenue Potential

4,000 businesses at $49/mo average = $2.4M ARR.

## Existing Competitors

Canny ($79+/mo, feature requests only), UserVoice ($699+/mo), Productboard ($20+/user/mo = expensive for teams), Delighted (NPS only). No tool aggregates ALL feedback channels for SMBs at an affordable price.

## Competitive Moat

Multi-source integrations create a comprehensive data asset. AI theme detection improves with volume. Feature voting board creates user engagement that reinforces the product. Trend data over time becomes increasingly valuable and hard to replicate.
