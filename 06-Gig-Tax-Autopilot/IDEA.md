## The Problem
59 million Americans performed freelance or gig work in 2023 (Upwork). 73% of gig workers say taxes are their biggest financial stressor (Stride Health survey). The average gig worker overpays taxes by $3,000-5,000/year because they miss deductions and don't make quarterly estimated payments (incurring IRS penalties). Most tax apps (TurboTax, H&R Block) handle filing but don't help with the ongoing quarterly estimation, deduction tracking, and set-aside calculations that gig workers need year-round.

## The Solution
1. Connect gig platform accounts (Uber, Lyft, DoorDash, Instacart, Etsy, Upwork) + bank accounts
2. Auto-import 1099 income in real-time
3. Automatic mileage tracking via phone GPS (with trip classification: business vs. personal)
4. AI-powered deduction finder: scans expenses for gig-specific write-offs (phone bill %, home office, supplies, platform fees)
5. Real-time quarterly tax estimate with "set aside this much" alerts
6. One-click IRS quarterly payment (via EFTPS integration or payment voucher generation)
7. Year-end tax document package ready for filing
