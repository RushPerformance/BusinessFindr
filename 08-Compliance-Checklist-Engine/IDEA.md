## The Problem
Small businesses face an average of $83,019/year in regulatory compliance costs (National Association of Manufacturers). 87% of small business owners say compliance is their most time-consuming non-revenue activity. The challenge isn't that regulations are hard to follow — it's that business owners don't even know which regulations apply to them. A restaurant in California has different requirements than one in Texas: health permits, food handler certifications, ADA compliance, liquor licenses, grease trap inspections, fire suppression system checks, tip reporting, and more. Most small business owners discover compliance gaps only when they get fined.

## The Solution
1. Business owner enters industry, state, city, number of employees, revenue range
2. App generates a comprehensive compliance checklist with every applicable regulation
3. Each item has: what it is, why it matters, how to comply, deadline, renewal frequency
4. Calendar view with upcoming deadlines and automated reminders (email/SMS)
5. Document upload: attach licenses, permits, certifications to their checklist items
6. Completion tracking with compliance score (for peace of mind and insurance purposes)
7. Updates when regulations change (pulled from state/federal databases)
