# Freelancer Invoice Chaser

> Automated payment follow-up system for freelancers: sends escalating reminder sequences, tracks outstanding invoices, and provides late-fee enforcement templates.

## The Problem
Freelancers in the US are owed $50,000+ in unpaid invoices on average over their career (Freelancers Union). 71% of freelancers have trouble getting paid at least once in their career. The average freelance invoice is paid 13 days late. Chasing payments is awkward, time-consuming, and emotionally draining — most freelancers hate doing it and either give up or spend hours writing polite-but-firm follow-up emails. Existing invoicing tools (FreshBooks, Wave) send basic reminders but don't provide strategic escalation sequences, late-fee calculation, or pre-written scripts for difficult conversations.

## The Solution
1. Import invoices from FreshBooks, QuickBooks, Wave, Stripe, or create them in-app
2. Set payment terms and automated follow-up sequences:
   - Day 1 overdue: Friendly reminder
   - Day 7: Firmer follow-up
   - Day 14: Late fee notice
   - Day 30: Final notice with collections warning
3. All emails customizable but pre-written with professional templates
4. Late fee calculator (state-specific maximums)
5. Dashboard: total outstanding, aging report, client payment reliability scores
6. "Awkward conversation" scripts for phone/video calls
7. Small claims court filing guide for amounts under state limits

## Existing Alternatives
- **FreshBooks**: $17/mo minimum, invoicing + accounting (overkill for just chasing payments)
- **Wave**: Free invoicing with basic reminders, no escalation logic
- **Bonsai**: $21/mo, full freelance suite (contracts + invoicing + taxes)
- **PayPal invoicing**: Basic, no follow-up automation
- **Manual emails**: The most common approach (copy-paste follow-up templates)

## Why This Wins
- Laser focus on the payment collection problem, not full invoicing/accounting
- Escalation sequences are the core product — this is what freelancers actually struggle with
- Pre-written templates remove the emotional burden of chasing money
- Client reliability scores help freelancers avoid bad clients in the future
- Low price point makes it accessible to freelancers who are cash-strapped
