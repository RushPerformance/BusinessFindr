## The Problem
National average rent increased 25% from 2020-2024. 44% of US renters (22M households) spend more than 30% of income on rent (Harvard Joint Center for Housing Studies). When landlords send rent increase notices, most tenants don't know whether the increase is reasonable, what comparable units charge, or that they have leverage to negotiate. Many just accept or move — both costly options. Rent control laws vary wildly by state/city and most tenants don't know their rights.

## The Solution
1. Enter current rent, location, unit details, and proposed increase amount
2. App pulls comparable rental listings within a 1-mile radius for context
3. Shows local rent increase trends and averages (is this increase above/below market?)
4. Checks applicable rent control/stabilization laws for the jurisdiction
5. Generates a customized negotiation letter/email to the landlord
6. Provides phone call scripts with specific data points to cite
7. Alternative: shows cheaper comparable listings if negotiation fails
8. Monitors future rent trends and alerts before lease renewal
