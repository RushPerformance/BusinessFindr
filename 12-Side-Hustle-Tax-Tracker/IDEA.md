## The Problem
36% of Americans have a side hustle (Bankrate, 2023). Most earn $1K-$20K/year from Etsy, reselling, tutoring, content creation, etc. They don't need QuickBooks — they need to know one thing: "how much do I owe in taxes on this extra income?" Most side hustlers are surprised by a tax bill in April because they didn't set money aside. Many don't know the $400 self-employment tax threshold, quarterly payment requirements, or which expenses are deductible.

## The Solution
1. Log side hustle income (manual or bank connection)
2. Log deductible expenses with receipt photos
3. Real-time tax liability estimate (federal + state + self-employment tax)
4. "Set aside" alert: tells you exactly how much to save from each payment
5. Quarterly payment reminders with pre-filled vouchers
6. Year-end summary formatted for Schedule C/SE
7. Education: plain-English explainers on self-employment tax rules
