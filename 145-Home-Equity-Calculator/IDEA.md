## Problem

Home equity represents the single largest asset for 65% of American homeowners, totaling $35 trillion collectively — yet most homeowners check their equity once a year at best and have only a vague sense of their largest financial asset. Zillow's Zestimate updates provide a rough home value, but translating that into actionable equity calculations requires subtracting mortgage balance, understanding LTV ratios, and factoring in market trends — math most homeowners don't do. Meanwhile, $1.5 trillion in HELOC borrowing capacity goes untapped because homeowners don't realize how much equity they've built. On the flip side, 42% of homeowners who take out HELOCs don't comparison shop, leaving an average of $2,400 on the table in interest costs over 5 years. Renovation decisions are similarly uninformed: homeowners spend $420 billion annually on improvements without understanding which projects actually increase home value vs. which are pure consumption.

---

## Solution

1. **Real-Time Equity Dashboard** — Connects to your mortgage servicer and local home value data to display current equity, LTV ratio, and equity growth over time.
2. **Equity Growth Projections** — Models future equity based on mortgage amortization schedule, local appreciation trends, and extra payment scenarios.
3. **HELOC/Home Equity Loan Comparison** — Compare HELOC offers from multiple lenders based on your specific equity position, credit profile, and borrowing needs.
4. **Renovation ROI Calculator** — Estimates how specific renovations (kitchen remodel, bathroom update, deck addition) would change your home's value based on local market data.
5. **Extra Payment Impact** — Shows how additional mortgage payments accelerate equity building with month-by-month projections and total interest saved.
6. **PMI Elimination Tracker** — Tracks progress toward 20% equity (80% LTV) for homeowners still paying private mortgage insurance, with estimated PMI elimination date.
7. **Wealth Building View** — Contextualizes home equity within overall net worth, comparing equity growth rate to alternative investments like stock market returns.

## Existing Alternatives

1. **Zillow/Redfin** — Provide home value estimates but no equity calculations, no mortgage integration, and no financial planning features.
2. **Rocket Mortgage/LoanDepot Apps** — Show mortgage balance and payment tracking but only for their own loans, with no equity optimization or HELOC comparison.
3. **Mint/Personal Capital** — Track home value as a net worth line item but no dedicated equity analysis, renovation ROI, or HELOC tools.
4. **Bankrate HELOC Calculator** — Simple web calculator for HELOC payments but no personalized analysis, no lender comparison, and no ongoing tracking.
5. **Financial advisors** — Provide holistic equity advice but charge $200+/hour and most homeowners don't have one.

## Why This Wins

- **Single-asset deep dive** — While net worth apps treat home equity as one line item, this app treats it as the primary financial asset it actually is for most Americans.
- **Actionable HELOC comparison** — No consumer app makes it easy to comparison shop HELOCs based on your actual equity position with real lender offers.
- **Renovation ROI data** — Translating renovation costs into equity impact gives homeowners a financial framework for improvement decisions, not just aesthetic.
- **PMI elimination tracking** — Millions of homeowners pay $100-300/mo in PMI that could be eliminated sooner with strategic extra payments — this app makes that visible.
- **Lead generation model** — Free tier attracts homeowners; premium tier and HELOC referral partnerships create dual revenue streams.
