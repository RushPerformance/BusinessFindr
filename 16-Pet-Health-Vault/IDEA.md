# Pet Health Vault

> Digital health record and care tracker for pets: vaccination schedules, medication logs, vet visit history, and breed-specific health alerts — shareable across vets and caregivers.

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Category | Pet Care |
| Target Audience | Mass Market — pet owners (67% of US households, 86.9M homes) |
| Monetization | Freemium — free for 1 pet, $3.99/mo for multiple pets + premium features |
| Recession Resistance | Medium-High — Americans spent $136.8B on pets in 2022 (APPA); pet spending is historically recession-resistant as owners treat pets as family |
| Solo-Dev MVP Timeline | 5 weeks |
| Composite Score | 75/100 |

## The Problem
67% of US households own a pet (APPA, 2023). Pet owners visit vets 2-3 times per year and struggle to keep track of vaccination dates, medication schedules, and health histories across multiple vets. When switching vets, boarding a pet, or traveling, pet owners can't easily share health records. 78M US dogs need annual rabies/DHPP vaccines, but 40% of pets are behind on vaccinations (AVMA) partly because owners forget or lose track. Multi-pet households (35% of pet owners have 2+ pets) struggle even more.

## The Solution
1. Create pet profiles with breed, age, weight, microchip number, allergies
2. Log vet visits with notes, diagnoses, and treatment plans
3. Vaccination tracker with automated reminders before due dates
4. Medication schedule with dosage reminders
5. Breed-specific health alerts (e.g., "Golden Retrievers should be screened for hip dysplasia by age 2")
6. Shareable health card: QR code or link for vets, groomers, boarders, pet sitters
7. Weight and wellness tracking over time with charts
8. Emergency vet locator with after-hours availability

## Existing Alternatives
- **PetDesk**: Vet-side scheduling app, not patient/owner-side health records
- **Petly (IDEXX)**: Vet-initiated portal, limited to IDEXX partner clinics
- **11pets**: Basic tracker, poor UX, limited features
- **Spreadsheets/paper**: Most common approach (folders of printed vet records)
- **Photos of vet records**: Extremely common, unsearchable

## Why This Wins
- Owner-controlled records (vs. vet-controlled like PetDesk/Petly) mean portability across any vet
- Breed-specific health alerts are a unique educational feature
- Shareable QR health card is practical for boarding, travel, and pet sitters
- Natural viral loop: multi-pet households, pet sitter/boarder sharing
- Pet parents are emotionally invested and willing to pay for peace of mind
