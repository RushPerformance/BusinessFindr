## Problem

U.S. consumers received an estimated 21.5 billion packages in 2023, and apartment residents — roughly 44 million households — receive a disproportionate share since they skew younger and shop online more frequently. Package theft costs Americans $12 billion annually, with apartments being the highest-risk location. Building managers spend 5-10 hours per week manually logging and managing packages. The average 200-unit building receives 80-120 packages daily. Unclaimed packages pile up, creating fire hazards and resident complaints. Roughly 15% of packages in apartment buildings go unclaimed for 7+ days because residents didn't know they arrived.

---

## Solution

1. **Quick Scan Logging** — Building staff scan package barcodes or snap photos to log deliveries in seconds.
2. **Instant Resident Notification** — Push notifications, SMS, or email to residents the moment their package is logged.
3. **Unclaimed Package Tracking** — Dashboard showing packages older than 48 hours with automated follow-up reminders.
4. **Pickup Confirmation** — Residents confirm pickup with a digital signature or QR code scan, creating an audit trail.
5. **Package Room Photo** — Photo documentation of package condition at arrival for damage dispute resolution.
6. **Building Analytics** — Volume trends, peak delivery days, average claim times, and staff efficiency metrics.
7. **Multi-Building Management** — Property management companies oversee multiple buildings from a single dashboard.
8. **Resident Directory Integration** — Auto-match packages to residents by name and unit number.

## Existing Alternatives

1. **Parcel Pending (Quadient)** — Smart package lockers, $15K-$50K installation cost plus monthly fees — prohibitively expensive for smaller buildings.
2. **Package Concierge** — Hardware locker solution, same cost barrier as Parcel Pending.
3. **Notifii Track** — Software package tracking for apartments, established competitor with enterprise focus and enterprise pricing.
4. **BuildingLink** — Comprehensive building management platform, package tracking is one of many features, overkill for smaller properties.
5. **Spreadsheets/Paper Logs** — The current solution for most buildings, error-prone, no notifications, no audit trail.

## Why This Wins

- **Software-only, no hardware** — competitors require $15K+ locker installations; this app needs only a smartphone.
- **$49/mo is accessible** — even small 20-unit buildings can justify the cost vs. $15K locker systems.
- **Reduces liability** — digital audit trail protects building management from package theft accusations.
- **Immediate time savings** — cuts staff package management time from 5-10 hours to 1-2 hours weekly.
- **Resident satisfaction** — instant notifications are the #1 requested amenity feature in resident satisfaction surveys.
