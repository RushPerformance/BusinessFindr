## Problem

While 14.2% of U.S. adults (36 million people) have tried meditation, only 8% maintain a regular practice. The primary reason for quitting is lack of accountability — 73% of meditation dropouts cite "forgetting" or "losing motivation." Popular meditation apps like Calm and Headspace have churn rates of 50-60% within 3 months despite premium content libraries. Research shows that meditation benefits require consistency — studies indicate 8+ weeks of daily practice to see measurable anxiety reduction. The biggest barrier isn't access to guided sessions (YouTube has millions for free) but rather the daily commitment mechanism.

---

## Solution

1. **One-Tap Timer** — Start meditating in under 2 seconds with preset durations (1, 3, 5, 10, 15, 20 minutes).
2. **Streak Counter** — Visual streak display showing consecutive days of practice, with milestone celebrations at 7, 30, 100, 365 days.
3. **Streak Protection** — One "freeze" per week to prevent losing a long streak due to illness or travel.
4. **Gentle Reminders** — Customizable daily notification at your preferred meditation time.
5. **Session History** — Calendar heatmap showing meditation days, total minutes, and longest streak.
6. **Ambient Sounds** — Optional background sounds (rain, ocean, forest, white noise) — no guided content, just atmosphere.
7. **Widget Support** — Home screen widget showing current streak and one-tap session start.
