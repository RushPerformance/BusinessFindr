## Problem

There are 18 million Americans living with roommates, and the number is growing as housing costs rise — average rent now exceeds $2,000/month in 50+ U.S. metro areas. Bad roommate matches are the #1 reason for lease-breaking, costing $1,500-$5,000 in early termination fees. Studies show that 63% of roommate conflicts stem from lifestyle incompatibilities (cleanliness, noise, schedules) rather than financial issues. Existing roommate finder platforms (Craigslist, Facebook groups, SpareRoom) primarily match on budget and location, with minimal lifestyle compatibility filtering. The result: 40% of roommate arrangements end before the lease is up.

---

## Solution

1. **Lifestyle Compatibility Quiz** — 25-question assessment covering sleep schedule, cleanliness standards, noise tolerance, cooking habits, pet preferences, and social patterns.
2. **Compatibility Score** — Algorithm-generated score (0-100%) for each potential match showing overall fit and category breakdowns.
3. **Deal-Breaker Filters** — Hard filters for non-negotiables (smoking, pets, overnight guests, substance use).
4. **Verification System** — Optional ID verification and social media linking for safety.
5. **In-App Messaging** — Secure chat to discuss details before exchanging personal information.
6. **Roommate Agreement Template** — Pre-built template covering chores, guests, quiet hours, and shared expenses based on both parties' quiz responses.
7. **Budget Matching** — Traditional budget and location filters layered with lifestyle compatibility.
