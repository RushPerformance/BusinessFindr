## Problem

Americans attend an estimated 100 million potluck events per year — office parties, church dinners, school events, neighborhood gatherings, and family reunions. The #1 complaint: too many of the same dish. Without coordination, 4 people bring potato salad and nobody brings a main dish. Group text coordination is chaotic — messages get buried, people forget to respond, and the organizer has no central view. SignUpGenius handles this but requires account creation, is cluttered with features for formal volunteer scheduling, and feels like enterprise software for a casual meal. Most potlucks end up with text thread chaos or a hastily shared Google Doc.

---

## Solution

1. **One-Tap Event Creation** — Create a potluck in 15 seconds: event name, date, and number of guests.
2. **Category Slots** — Pre-defined categories (appetizers, mains, sides, desserts, drinks) with target quantities based on guest count.
3. **Claim System** — Guests claim a slot with their dish name; claimed categories show as filled to prevent duplicates.
4. **No Account Required** — Share via link; guests claim slots without creating an account.
5. **Dietary Tag System** — Flag dishes as vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, nut-free, etc., with visual dietary coverage indicator.
6. **Serving Size Calculator** — Recommends portion sizing based on total guest count so nobody under- or over-prepares.
7. **Event Reminders** — Automatic reminder to claimed participants 24 hours before the event.

## Existing Alternatives

1. **SignUpGenius** — Market leader for group signups but bloated with volunteer management features; requires accounts.
2. **Google Docs/Sheets** — Freeform and flexible but no duplicate prevention, no categories, ugly on mobile.
3. **Group Text / WhatsApp** — Chaotic, messages get buried, no central list, no duplicate checking.
4. **Evite** — Event invitations with some signup features but primarily for RSVPs, not dish coordination.
5. **Meal Train** — Designed for meal delivery coordination (illness, new baby), not social potlucks.
