## The Problem
Restaurants waste 4-10% of purchased food before it reaches customers, costing the average independent restaurant $25,000-$75,000/year (ReFED, 2023). Total US restaurant food waste: $25B annually. Most restaurants track waste informally or not at all. Large chains use enterprise waste management systems (LeanPath, Winnow) that cost $500-2,000/mo and require IoT hardware. Independent restaurants (70% of US restaurants) have no affordable solution.

## The Solution
1. Daily waste log: staff enter waste by category (overproduction, spoilage, prep trim, plate waste) using a simple tablet interface
2. Cost analytics dashboard: total waste in dollars, waste as % of food cost, trends over time
3. Category breakdown: which ingredients are wasted most, which stations/shifts have most waste
4. AI ordering recommendations: "You consistently over-order romaine lettuce by 15% on Mondays"
5. Par level optimizer: suggests prep quantities based on historical sales + waste data
6. Weekly waste report emailed to owner/manager
7. Waste reduction goal tracking with team accountability
