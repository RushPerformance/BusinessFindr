## Problem

There are 44 million renter households in the United States. The average renter has a 12-month lease, and missing key deadlines costs real money: failing to give 60-day notice can trigger automatic renewal at higher rates or month-to-month surcharges of $200-$400/month. An estimated 30% of renters miss their lease renewal notification deadline, losing negotiating leverage. Security deposit disputes total $4.5 billion annually, with renters forfeiting an average of $600 per move-out due to undocumented conditions and missed cleaning requirements. First-time renters are especially vulnerable, with 45% reporting confusion about their lease obligations and timelines.

---

## Solution

1. **Visual Countdown** — Clear countdown timer showing days remaining on your lease with milestone markers.
2. **Critical Date Alerts** — Notifications for key deadlines: notice period start, renewal decision deadline, move-out date.
3. **Renewal Negotiation Guide** — Tips and scripts for negotiating rent increases, based on market data and lease terms.
4. **Move-Out Checklist** — Comprehensive checklist covering cleaning, repairs, utility transfers, mail forwarding, and key return.
5. **Security Deposit Protector** — Photo documentation guide for move-in and move-out condition to maximize deposit return.
6. **Lease Terms Summary** — Input key lease terms (notice period, pet deposit, early termination fee) for quick reference.
7. **Next Steps Guide** — Decision framework for renew vs. move with local rent trend data.
