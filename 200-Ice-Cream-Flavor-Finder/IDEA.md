---

## Problem

The U.S. ice cream market generates $9.8 billion in retail and food service sales annually. Americans consume 1.5 billion gallons of ice cream per year, with the average person eating 23 pounds annually. Yet finding a specific flavor at a nearby shop is surprisingly difficult. Shops rotate flavors daily or weekly, seasonal flavors appear and disappear without notice, and dietary-restricted customers (dairy-free, vegan, sugar-free) have no way to verify availability before visiting. Artisan ice cream shops — over 2,700 in the U.S. — create limited-batch flavors that sell out in hours. Customers make wasted trips to discover their desired flavor isn't available, and shops have no way to broadcast what's in the case right now.

---

## Solution

1. **Flavor Search** — Search for a specific flavor (salted caramel, mint chip, lavender honey) and find which nearby shops have it today.
2. **Shop Profiles** — Ice cream shop listings with full current flavor menus, hours, pricing, and photos.
3. **Dietary Filters** — Filter by dairy-free, vegan, sugar-free, nut-free, and other dietary needs.
4. **Real-Time Availability** — Shops update their daily flavor roster; customers see what's in the case right now.
5. **Flavor Alerts** — Get notified when your favorite seasonal or limited-batch flavor becomes available nearby.
6. **Community Ratings** — Rate flavors and shops, building a flavor recommendation engine over time.
7. **Flavor Map** — Discover new shops and flavors by browsing a map of nearby options.
8. **Seasonal Calendar** — Track when seasonal flavors (pumpkin, peppermint, mango) typically appear at your favorite shops.

## Existing Alternatives

1. **Google Maps** — Lists ice cream shops but no flavor-level data or real-time availability.
2. **Yelp** — Reviews of ice cream shops but no current flavor information or dietary filtering.
3. **Instagram** — Many shops post flavor boards daily, but fragmented across individual accounts.
4. **Shop Websites** — Some shops list flavors online, but most don't update daily or have mobile-friendly sites.

---

## Why This Wins

- **Flavor-level granularity** — no one else provides real-time, flavor-specific availability data for ice cream.
- **Dietary restriction solution** — dairy-free and allergy-conscious customers have zero good options for verifying availability.
- **Shop-friendly value proposition** — shops get free marketing and foot traffic by participating.
- **Seasonal engagement** — summer drives peak usage with natural engagement from March through September.
- **Community and joy** — ice cream is inherently positive; the app benefits from associating with happiness and treats.
