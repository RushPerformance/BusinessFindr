## The Problem
The average US household pays $2,060/year in electricity alone, plus gas, water, and internet. Utility billing errors affect 1 in 5 bills (Public Utility Commission studies). Most consumers are on sub-optimal rate plans — time-of-use plans, budget billing, or competitive energy providers could save 10-20%. But comparing utility plans is confusing, and most people never switch. Internet bills creep up $5-15/year through hidden price increases most customers never notice.

## The Solution
1. Upload utility bills (photo/PDF) or connect accounts where available
2. AI extracts charges, usage patterns, rate structures
3. Error detection: flags unusual charges, double-billing, incorrect rate tier
4. Rate plan comparison: shows savings from switching to time-of-use, budget billing, or competitive providers
5. Usage anomaly detection: "Your water usage spiked 300% — possible leak"
6. Internet bill negotiation: detects price creep and generates complaint scripts/emails
7. Monthly savings dashboard: total saved since joining
8. Seasonal tips: "Your AC is costing $X/day — here's how to cut it"

## Existing Alternatives
- **Arcadia**: Clean energy matching, not bill auditing
- **Sense/Emporia**: Hardware energy monitors ($200+), not bill analysis
- **Trim/Rocket Money**: Have bill negotiation but as a side feature, not core
- **BillShark**: Human negotiators taking 40% of savings
- **Manual review**: Rare, most people just auto-pay and never look

## Why This Wins
- No hardware required (unlike Sense/Emporia)
- Self-service approach is cheaper than negotiation services (BillShark)
- Error detection is a unique angle — finding billing mistakes is immediate, tangible value
- Multi-utility approach (electric + gas + water + internet) is more comprehensive than competitors
- Strong word-of-mouth: "this app found a $47 billing error"
