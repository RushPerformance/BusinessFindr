> Helps homeowners compare contractor bids apples-to-apples by normalizing line items, flagging missing scope, and showing local price benchmarks.

## The Problem
The "get 3 quotes" advice for home projects is universal, but comparing contractor bids is nearly impossible. One contractor quotes a lump sum, another breaks it into 50 line items, a third includes things the others exclude. Homeowners don't know if a $15,000 bathroom remodel bid is fair or a ripoff. 67% of homeowners say comparing contractor quotes is their biggest frustration with renovation projects (Houzz, 2023). The average homeowner overpays by 10-25% simply because they can't compare bids effectively.

## The Solution
1. Upload or enter contractor bids (photo/PDF/manual entry)
2. AI normalizes bids into comparable line items (materials, labor, permits, cleanup, etc.)
3. Side-by-side comparison highlighting what's included/excluded in each bid
4. "Missing scope" alerts: "Contractor A didn't include dumpster removal — Contractor B did"
5. Local price benchmarks: is this price above or below average for your zip code?
6. Red flag detection: "This bid has no warranty mentioned" or "Markup on materials seems high at 45%"
7. Decision matrix with weighted scoring (price, warranty, timeline, reviews)
8. Questions to ask each contractor based on bid gaps
