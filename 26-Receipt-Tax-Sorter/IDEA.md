# Receipt Tax Sorter

> Scan receipts year-round, auto-categorize by tax deduction type (business, medical, charitable, home office), and export IRS-ready summaries at tax time.

## The Problem
28% of US taxpayers itemize deductions. Self-employed individuals, landlords, and medical spenders need to categorize thousands of receipts by tax category. The IRS requires records for 3-7 years. Most people use shoeboxes, folders, or scattered photos. At tax time, they spend 5-15 hours sorting receipts — or miss deductions entirely. The average taxpayer misses $500-1,000 in legitimate deductions due to poor record-keeping.

## The Solution
1. Snap photos of receipts (paper or forward email receipts)
2. OCR extracts vendor, amount, date, and items purchased
3. AI auto-categorizes by IRS deduction type (Schedule A, C, E line items)
4. Manual override and custom categories
5. Year-end export: PDF summary organized by Schedule/line item, ready for CPA or TurboTax
6. Receipt image storage (IRS-compliant digital records)
7. Deduction tracker: running total of deductions by category with "potential savings" estimate
8. Mileage log integration for business travel

## Why This Wins
- Personal tax focus (not business expense reports like Expensify)
- IRS Schedule line-item categorization is unique — no competitor auto-maps to tax forms
- Lower price than Shoeboxed/Neat with better AI categorization
- "Potential savings" estimate motivates continued use throughout the year
- Tax season marketing creates predictable demand spikes
