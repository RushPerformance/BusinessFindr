## The Problem
Small claims court handles disputes under $5,000-$25,000 (varies by state). 10+ million cases are filed annually. The process is designed to be lawyer-free, but most people are intimidated by it: they don't know their state's rules, how to file, what evidence to bring, or how to present their case. Many valid claims go unfiled because the process feels too confusing. Filing fees are $30-75 but potential recovery is thousands.

## The Solution
1. Case evaluator: describe your dispute, app tells you if small claims court is appropriate
2. State-specific rules: filing deadlines, monetary limits, where to file, service requirements
3. Document generator: complaint form, demand letter, evidence list template
4. Evidence coach: "Bring screenshots of text messages, the contract, and bank statements"
5. Hearing prep: what to say, how to present evidence, common judge questions
6. Day-of checklist: what to wear, bring, and expect
7. Post-judgment guide: how to collect if you win
8. Defendant mode: guides defendants through responding to claims
