## The Problem
44 million US adults have roommates (Census Bureau). Roommate conflicts are the #1 reason for early lease breaks (Apartment List). Top conflicts: uneven chores (67%), unpaid shared bills (54%), noise (48%), and guests (42%). Splitwise handles expense splitting but nothing else. No app addresses the full roommate experience: shared finances, chore fairness, house rules, and communication.

## The Solution
1. Expense splitting with auto-calculated owed amounts and payment reminders
2. Chore rotation schedule with fairness scoring and swap requests
3. Shared calendar: guest notifications, quiet hours, maintenance visits
4. House rules agreement: collaboratively set and reference rules
5. Conflict resolution: structured templates for raising issues without confrontation
6. Supply tracker: who bought toilet paper last? (tracks shared supply purchases)
7. Move-out calculator: security deposit split, final utility settlement

## Existing Alternatives

- **Group texts/WhatsApp**: Communication only, no tracking
- **Sticky notes**: The current "technology" for chore reminders

## Why This Wins
- Holistic approach (expenses + chores + communication + conflict resolution) is unique
- Splitwise only does expenses — this does everything else too
- Conflict resolution templates are a novel, high-value feature
- Natural viral coefficient: every user invites 1-4 roommates
- College and young professional demographics are highly mobile and digitally native
