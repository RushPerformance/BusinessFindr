# Medication Price Shopper

> Compares prescription drug prices across nearby pharmacies and shows manufacturer coupons, patient assistance programs, and generic alternatives to slash medication costs.

| Field | Detail |

| Recession Resistance | High — medication is non-discretionary; price sensitivity increases during downturns |
| Solo-Dev MVP Timeline | 6 weeks |

## The Problem
29 million Americans are uninsured and millions more are underinsured with high deductibles. Prescription drug prices vary by 300-1,000% between pharmacies for the same medication. A 90-day supply of Lipitor can cost $17 at Costco or $450 at CVS. Most patients don't know prices vary this much, and they don't know about GoodRx-style coupons, manufacturer patient assistance programs, or $4 generic lists at Walmart.

## The Solution
1. Enter medication name and dosage
2. See prices at all pharmacies within chosen radius
3. Show applicable coupons and discount cards (GoodRx, RxSaver, manufacturer coupons)
4. Patient assistance program finder (for low-income/uninsured patients)
5. Generic/therapeutic alternative suggestions with doctor-shareable comparison
6. Refill reminders timed to when you'll run out
7. Pharmacy transfer assistance: switch to cheaper pharmacy with one tap
8. Annual medication cost report

## Existing Alternatives
- **GoodRx**: Dominant player, free price comparison + coupons
- **RxSaver**: Similar to GoodRx, smaller scale
- **Cost Plus Drugs (Mark Cuban)**: Online pharmacy with transparent pricing
- **Blink Health**: Discounted prices but limited pharmacy network
- **Insurance formulary tools**: Only for insured patients

## Why This Wins
- Patient assistance program finder differentiates from GoodRx (which doesn't cover these)
- Generic/therapeutic alternative suggestions go beyond price to actual drug substitution options
- GoodRx has become so dominant that they can set prices; a competitor with different pharmacy partnerships could undercut
- Focus on uninsured/underinsured is a clearer niche than GoodRx's broad market approach
