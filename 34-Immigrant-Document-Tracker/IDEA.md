## The Problem
45 million immigrants live in the US, and millions more are in various stages of the immigration process. US immigration is extraordinarily complex: there are 180+ visa types, each with different requirements, timelines, and renewal deadlines. Missing a single deadline — a visa renewal, an I-485 filing window, a biometrics appointment — can result in deportation, loss of work authorization, or starting the multi-year process over. Immigration attorneys charge $3,000-$15,000 per case, and most immigrants manage their own paperwork. USCIS processing times are opaque, case statuses are hard to track, and policy changes happen frequently.

## The Solution
1. Visa type selector: identify your visa category and see all requirements, timelines, and deadlines
2. Document checklist: every form and supporting document needed, with completion tracking
3. Deadline alerts: renewal dates, filing windows, travel restrictions, and status expiration warnings
4. Case status tracker: auto-check USCIS case status and notify on updates
5. Processing time estimator: current wait times by visa type, service center, and field office
6. Policy change alerts: notifications when immigration rules change for your visa category
7. Document vault: secure storage for passports, I-94s, approval notices, and tax returns
8. Green card milestone tracker: from application to interview to approval
9. Travel advisory: "Can I travel internationally right now?" based on your current status

## Existing Alternatives
- **USCIS website**: Official but confusing, no personalized tracking
- **Lawfully**: Immigration tracker app, but limited features and inconsistent updates
- **VisaJourney**: Community forums with timelines, not a tracking tool
- **Immigration attorneys**: Expensive, not everyone can afford one
- **Excel spreadsheets**: Common DIY approach, no automation or alerts
- **Boundless**: Helps with specific applications but not ongoing tracking

## Why This Wins
- Policy change alerts are uniquely valuable — immigration rules shift frequently and the consequences of missing changes are severe
- Processing time estimator reduces anxiety and helps with life planning (job offers, leases, travel)
- Travel advisory feature prevents the common and devastating mistake of traveling when it voids your status
- Emotional urgency: immigration deadlines are life-altering, creating strong willingness to pay
- Underserved market: immigrants are digitally savvy but have few purpose-built tools
