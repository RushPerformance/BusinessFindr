## The Problem
Noise complaints are the #1 neighbor dispute in the US. 45% of apartment renters report noise as a significant issue (National Apartment Association). When noise becomes chronic — barking dogs, late-night parties, construction, stomping upstairs neighbors — residents need documentation to take action. Landlords, HOAs, and municipalities require evidence: dates, times, duration, and severity. But most people just have vague complaints ("it happens all the time"), which landlords and authorities dismiss. Without systematic documentation, chronic noise sufferers feel trapped.

## The Solution
1. One-tap noise logging: record decibel level with timestamp and duration using phone microphone
2. Noise event calendar: visual timeline of all documented noise incidents
3. Decibel measurement: calibrated sound level meter with local noise ordinance thresholds
4. Evidence package generator: PDF report with dates, times, decibel readings, and duration — ready for landlord, HOA, or city
5. Noise ordinance lookup: local quiet hours and decibel limits for your municipality
6. Complaint letter templates: pre-written letters for landlords, HOAs, and city noise enforcement
7. Audio snippet recording: short recordings (with privacy disclaimers) as supporting evidence
8. Escalation guide: when to involve mediation, housing authority, or legal action

## Existing Alternatives
- **NIOSH SLM app (NIOSH/CDC)**: Free decibel meter, but no documentation or complaint features
- **Decibel X**: Sound meter app, measurement only, no evidence building
- **Notes app + voice memos**: Manual, unstructured, not formatted for complaints
- **311 apps**: File complaints but don't help you build a documented case
- **Attorneys**: $200-400/hr for noise-related legal action

## Why This Wins
- Evidence package generator is the killer feature — transforms scattered complaints into credible documentation
- Noise ordinance lookup with decibel thresholds tells users whether their complaint is legally actionable
- Both subscription and one-time pricing options accommodate different user situations (ongoing vs. one-time dispute)
- Escalation guide helps users understand their options beyond just complaining
- High emotional urgency: chronic noise affects sleep, health, and quality of life — people are motivated to solve it
