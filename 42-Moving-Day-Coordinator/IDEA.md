## The Problem
28 million Americans move each year (Census Bureau). Moving is consistently rated one of life's most stressful events, yet most people plan moves with scattered notes and memory. The average move requires 50+ individual tasks: forwarding mail, transferring utilities, updating addresses with banks/insurance/DMV, packing, hiring movers, cleaning, and dozens more. People inevitably forget critical steps — resulting in missed mail, billing disruptions, lost deposits, and unnecessary stress. The moving industry focuses on the physical move, not the administrative chaos surrounding it.

## The Solution
1. Personalized moving timeline: based on your move date, get a week-by-week task schedule
2. Master checklist: 80+ categorized tasks (utilities, address changes, packing, cleaning, etc.)
3. Address change hub: list of every entity that needs your new address with direct links to update
4. Utility transfer manager: schedule disconnects at old address and connects at new address
5. Box inventory: number and label boxes by room, searchable when unpacking ("Where did I pack the blender?")
6. Moving company comparison: get quotes and track booking details
7. Security deposit documentation: move-in/move-out photo checklists to protect your deposit
8. Post-move checklist: things to do in your first week (locate breaker box, change locks, meet neighbors)
