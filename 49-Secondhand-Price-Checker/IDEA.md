## The Problem
The US secondhand market is worth $227 billion and growing 5x faster than traditional retail (ThredUp). Millions of people sell items on eBay, Facebook Marketplace, Poshmark, and Mercari — but pricing is the hardest part. Sellers either price too high (items don't sell) or too low (leaving money on the table). Currently, pricing requires manually searching completed listings on each platform. Thrift shoppers and garage sale flippers need instant price checks to decide whether a $5 find is worth $50 online. There's no single tool that aggregates real-time resale values across all major platforms.

## The Solution
1. Barcode scan: scan any product barcode to see current resale value across platforms
2. Photo search: take a photo of an item (clothing, vintage, unboxed items) for AI-powered price lookup
3. Cross-platform pricing: see what the item sells for on eBay, Facebook Marketplace, Poshmark, Mercari, and Depop
4. Sold vs. listed prices: show actual sold prices (not just asking prices) for accurate valuation
5. Condition-based pricing: estimated value for new, like-new, good, and fair condition
6. Best platform recommendation: "This item sells best on Poshmark" based on category and price point
7. Listing optimizer: suggested title, description, and price for maximum sale probability
8. Profit calculator: subtract platform fees, shipping costs, and your purchase price to see net profit
9. Inventory tracker: log items you've purchased to resell with cost basis and target price

## Existing Alternatives
- **eBay sold listings**: Manual search, eBay-only, time-consuming
- **Poshmark Closet Tools**: Poshmark-only, limited pricing data
- **WorthPoint**: $24.99/mo, antiques/collectibles focused, expensive
- **Terapeak**: eBay-specific market research tool
- **Google Lens**: Identifies items but doesn't show resale prices
- **Facebook Marketplace search**: Manual, no historical sold data

## Why This Wins
- Cross-platform aggregation is the key differentiator — no tool shows resale value across all major platforms simultaneously
- Sold price data (not asking prices) provides accurate real-world valuation
- Barcode + photo scanning enables instant price checks at thrift stores and garage sales
- Profit calculator with fee deduction gives sellers the number that actually matters (net profit)
- Huge and growing addressable market driven by sustainability trends and economic pressures
