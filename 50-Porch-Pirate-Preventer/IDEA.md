## The Problem
1.7 billion packages are delivered to US homes annually, and an estimated 49 million Americans have had a package stolen (Security.org). Package theft costs consumers and retailers $12 billion per year. 64% of Americans have experienced porch piracy at least once. Current solutions are reactive (doorbell cameras record theft after it happens) or expensive (Amazon Key, package lockers). The core problem is that packages sit unattended on porches for hours while residents are at work. Neighbors could help, but there's no structured system for coordinating package pickup among trusted people.

## The Solution
1. Delivery aggregator: track all incoming packages across carriers (USPS, UPS, FedEx, Amazon) in one feed
2. Arrival alerts: push notification the moment a package is delivered (before thieves spot it)
3. Trusted neighbor network: connect with verified neighbors who can grab your package when you're away
4. Pickup request: one-tap request to your trusted neighbor network when a delivery arrives and you're not home
5. Delivery instructions: shareable notes for drivers (hide behind planter, leave with neighbor at #42)
6. Theft report generator: if a package is stolen, generate a police report and carrier claim with one tap
7. Delivery time predictor: estimated delivery windows so you can plan to be home for high-value packages
8. Package photo verification: neighbors photo-confirm pickup for peace of mind

## Existing Alternatives
- **Ring/Nest doorbell cameras**: Record theft but don't prevent it ($100-250 + subscription)
- **Amazon Key**: In-garage delivery, Amazon packages only, requires hardware
- **Package lockers (Luxer One, Parcel Pending)**: Apartment-focused, not available for houses
- **Amazon Locker**: Requires driving to pickup location
- **USPS Informed Delivery**: USPS only, shows what's coming but no theft prevention
- **Nextdoor**: Can post about theft but no coordination tools

## Why This Wins
- Prevention focus (neighbor pickup) vs. detection focus (cameras record theft after the fact)
- Trusted neighbor network creates community bonds and mutual benefit
- Multi-carrier tracking in one place solves the "which app has my tracking info" fragmentation
- Theft report generator saves time and increases reporting rates (most thefts go unreported)
- Low-tech solution: no hardware required, unlike Ring, Amazon Key, or package lockers
