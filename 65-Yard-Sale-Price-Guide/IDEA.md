> Scan items and get suggested garage sale prices based on condition, age, and local demand — plus listing template generator for online marketplaces.

## The Problem
Americans hold 165,000+ garage sales every week (YardSaleSearch.com), and the secondhand market is worth $227B annually. The #1 challenge: pricing. Price too high and nothing sells; too low and you leave money on the table. Most people have no idea what a used Keurig, a 5-year-old IKEA bookshelf, or a set of golf clubs is worth at a garage sale. The difference between optimal and guessed pricing is typically 30-50% in lost revenue. Sellers also struggle to move unsold items to online marketplaces after the sale — creating listings from scratch on Facebook Marketplace, eBay, or Craigslist is tedious.

## The Solution
1. Item scanner: photograph or type an item name, get suggested garage sale price range
2. Condition adjustment: rate condition (like new, good, fair, poor) to fine-tune pricing
3. Local demand factor: adjusts prices based on your region's secondhand market activity
4. Pricing tiers: "Quick sale: $5 / Fair value: $12 / Wait for right buyer: $18"
5. Barcode scanning: scan product barcodes for instant identification and pricing
6. Listing template generator: one-tap creates a Facebook Marketplace or eBay listing with photo, title, description, and price
7. Sale day toolkit: printable price stickers, QR code for Venmo/CashApp, inventory checklist
8. Post-sale donation guide: what's tax-deductible, nearest donation centers, pickup scheduling

## Existing Alternatives
- **eBay completed listings**: Can research sold prices, but time-consuming and not garage-sale-specific
- **Decluttr**: Buys electronics at fixed prices, not a pricing guide
- **OfferUp/Letgo**: Marketplace apps but no pricing guidance
- **Google searching**: "How much is a used [item] worth?" — slow and inconsistent
- **Gut feeling**: How most garage sales are priced, with predictably poor results

## Why This Wins
- Photo-based scanning makes pricing instant instead of a 10-minute research task per item
- Three-tier pricing (quick sale / fair / premium) gives sellers strategic options
- Listing template generator turns unsold garage sale items into online listings in seconds
- $2.99 price point is an impulse purchase for anyone planning a sale
- Post-sale donation guide with tax deduction tracking adds ongoing value
- Viral potential: seller shares the app with every buyer at the sale
