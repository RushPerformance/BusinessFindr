## The Problem
Small groups are the backbone of church community — members who join a small group are 5x more likely to remain active in a church (LifeWay Research). But 60% of churchgoers have never joined one. The #1 barrier: finding the right group. Churches with 200+ members may have 20-50 small groups meeting at different times, locations, and focused on different topics (Bible study, parenting, grief, men's group, young professionals). Church staff spend hours manually matching members to groups using spreadsheets and personal knowledge. New members especially feel lost and unconnected — 82% of first-year church dropouts say they never formed meaningful relationships.

## The Solution
1. Member interest survey: short questionnaire about life stage, interests, schedule, and location preferences
2. Smart matching algorithm: recommends 3-5 best-fit groups based on survey results
3. Group directory: browsable catalog with descriptions, meeting times, location, leader bios, and open spots
4. One-click join requests: members can express interest directly; group leaders approve
5. Group communication hub: in-app messaging for scheduling changes, prayer requests, and updates
6. Attendance tracking for group leaders: see who's engaged and who might need outreach
7. Leader dashboard: manage rosters, track attendance trends, and request help from church staff
8. Church admin analytics: group health metrics, member engagement rates, unmatched member alerts

## Existing Alternatives
- **Planning Center Groups**: Part of the broader Planning Center suite ($14/mo+), but groups is basic
- **Church Community Builder (Pushpay)**: Enterprise church management, $200+/mo, overkill for small-mid churches
- **Realm by ACS**: Comprehensive but expensive and complex
- **Facebook Groups**: Free but no matching, no church integration, privacy concerns
- **Spreadsheets + announcements**: How most churches still manage small groups

## Why This Wins
- Smart matching is the unique feature: no church platform algorithmically connects members to groups
- $29.99/mo is affordable for small-mid size churches (100-1,000 members) underserved by enterprise tools
- Solves the new member connection problem that drives 82% of first-year dropout
- Group leaders get tools they actually need (communication, attendance) without a complex platform
- Church admin analytics show ROI: "Group members give 3x more and attend 2x more regularly"
- Natural expansion path: small groups today, volunteer coordination and events tomorrow
