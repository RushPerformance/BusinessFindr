## The Problem
77 million Americans volunteer annually (Bureau of Labor Statistics), logging 5.8 billion hours worth $195 billion. Yet tracking and verifying those hours is stuck in the paper age. High school students need verified hours for college applications and National Honor Society (typically 100-200 hours). Professionals track hours for corporate social responsibility programs. Court-ordered community service requires verified documentation. Currently, volunteers use paper sign-in sheets, email confirmations, and spreadsheets — losing records constantly. When it's time to compile hours for a college application or tax deduction, volunteers scramble to reconstruct months of service.

## The Solution
1. One-tap clock in/out: GPS-verified arrival and departure at volunteer locations
2. Automatic verification requests: sends confirmation emails to supervisors for one-click approval
3. Organization directory: find and connect with local volunteer opportunities
4. Tax deduction calculator: mileage, supplies, and uniform costs related to volunteering (IRS-compliant)
5. Resume/CV export: professionally formatted volunteer experience summary
6. College application export: formatted for Common App and other application platforms
7. Goal tracking: "42 of 100 required hours completed — on track to finish by April"
8. Impact dashboard: total hours, organizations served, estimated economic value of your service

## Existing Alternatives
- **Track it Forward**: Web-based volunteer hour tracking, organization-focused, clunky mobile experience
- **Helper Helper**: University volunteer tracking, not available to general public
- **Golden (by VolunteerHub)**: Organization management tool, not individual-focused
- **Paper timesheets**: Still the most common method at most volunteer organizations
- **Spreadsheets**: Manual tracking that gets abandoned after a few entries

## Why This Wins
- Automatic verification requests eliminate the "chase down your supervisor for a signature" problem
- College application export is a must-have for 3.5M+ high school seniors applying each year
- Tax deduction calculator surfaces money most volunteers don't know they can claim
- GPS-verified clock in/out creates trustworthy records for court-ordered service
- Free tier captures volume; students upgrade when college application deadlines approach
- Organization partnerships create distribution: "Track your hours at our nonprofit with this app"
