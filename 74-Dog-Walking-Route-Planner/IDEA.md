# Dog Walking Route Planner

> Generates dog walking routes with pet-friendly areas, water fountains, poop bag stations, and off-leash zones marked on the map.

## The Problem
65 million US households own dogs, and the average dog owner walks their dog 7-14 times per week. Yet most people walk the same 2-3 routes daily, leading to boredom for both dog and owner. Finding new routes is trial-and-error: Are dogs allowed on this trail? Is there a water fountain? Are there poop bag dispensers? Is this neighborhood dog-friendly? Where are the off-leash areas? Safety is another concern — certain areas have aggressive strays, heavy traffic, or no sidewalks. Dog walking apps focus on hiring walkers (Rover, Wag), not planning routes for owners who walk their own dogs.

## The Solution
1. Route generator: input walk distance and time, get optimized routes from your starting point
2. Pet-friendly POI layer: dog parks, off-leash areas, pet water fountains, poop bag stations
3. Crowdsourced safety ratings: "This route has heavy traffic at 5pm" or "aggressive stray dog reported on Oak St"
4. Walk tracker: distance, time, route GPS recording, and walk history
5. Explore mode: "Show me a new 30-minute route I haven't walked before"
6. Breed-appropriate walks: adjust distance and intensity for puppy, senior, high-energy, or small breed
7. Weather integration: "It's going to rain at 3pm — consider walking at 1pm instead"
8. Social feature: "3 other dog owners are walking at Riverside Park right now" for dog socialization

## Existing Alternatives
- **Rover/Wag**: Hire a dog walker, not a route planner for owner walks
- **AllTrails**: Hiking/trail app, not dog-specific (doesn't show dog-friendly trails or pet amenities)
- **Google Maps**: Can plan routes but no pet-specific POIs or dog park data
- **BarkHappy**: Dog-friendly places directory, limited to restaurants/stores, not walking routes
- **MapMyWalk**: General fitness walking tracker, no pet features

## Why This Wins
- "Explore new route" feature solves the daily boredom problem for both dog and owner
- Pet-friendly POI layer (water, bags, off-leash) doesn't exist in any general mapping app
- Breed-appropriate distance recommendations prevent over/under-exercising
- Crowdsourced safety ratings build community trust and unique data
- Dog owner community is passionate and vocal — high word-of-mouth potential
- Partnership opportunities with pet brands, dog parks, and pet-friendly businesses
