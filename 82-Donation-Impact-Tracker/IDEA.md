> Tracks charitable donations year-round, estimates tax deductions, and shows the measurable impact of each donation.

## The Problem
Americans donated $484 billion to charity in 2023 (Giving USA), yet most donors have no centralized record of their giving. At tax time, donors scramble to find receipts across email, paper files, and bank statements — the IRS requires documentation for any single donation over $250. Beyond tax tracking, 71% of donors say they want to know the impact of their giving (Fidelity Charitable), but charities provide inconsistent and vague impact reporting. Donors give to an average of 4.5 organizations per year but have no way to see their total giving picture, track it against a budget, or understand what their collective donations actually accomplished.

## The Solution
1. Donation log: record each donation with amount, date, organization, method (cash, check, stock, goods), and receipt photo
2. Tax deduction estimator: running tally of deductible donations with AGI-based deduction limit alerts
3. Impact translator: converts dollar amounts into tangible outcomes ("Your $50 to Feeding America provided 500 meals")
4. Charity effectiveness ratings pulled from GiveWell, Charity Navigator, and GuideStar
5. Annual giving budget: set a charitable giving target and track progress throughout the year
6. Tax-ready export: generate IRS-ready donation summary with all required documentation
7. In-kind donation tracker with fair market value estimator for donated goods
8. Giving history dashboard: year-over-year trends, causes supported, and total lifetime giving

## Existing Alternatives
- **ItsDeductible (TurboTax)**: Tracks in-kind donations but not cash donations, and only useful at tax time
- **Charity Navigator**: Rates charities but doesn't track personal donations
- **Daffy / Fidelity Charitable**: Donor-advised funds, but require $500+ minimum and are too complex for casual donors
- **Bank statements + email receipts**: The default "system," which is fragmented and unreliable
- **Spreadsheets**: Require manual entry and provide no impact translation or tax guidance

## Why This Wins
- Impact translation is the emotional hook — seeing "500 meals" instead of "$50" transforms the giving experience
- Year-round tracking eliminates the annual tax-time scramble for donation receipts
- Tax deduction estimator provides immediate financial value (average itemizing donor deducts $6,000+)
- Lower barrier than donor-advised funds — no minimums, no complex setup
- Giving budget feature encourages intentional generosity instead of reactive donating
