# Snow Removal Marketplace

> Connect homeowners with local snow plowers for on-demand driveway/sidewalk clearing with real-time tracking.

## The Problem
The US snow removal market is worth $22 billion annually, yet it's dominated by informal arrangements — neighbors with plows, handshake deals with landscapers, and last-minute scrambling during storms. 40% of homeowners in snow-belt states (Northeast, Midwest, Mountain West) handle their own snow removal despite physical risk — snow shoveling causes 11,500 injuries and 100 deaths per year (American Journal of Emergency Medicine). For elderly and disabled homeowners, finding reliable snow removal is critical but difficult. Most snow plowers don't have an online presence, don't take bookings, and can't be tracked in real-time. After a major storm, response times are unpredictable and prices spike.

## The Solution
1. On-demand booking: request snow removal with driveway size, snow depth, and service type (plow, shovel, salt)
2. Real-time tracking: see your plower en route and estimated arrival time
3. Provider profiles with ratings, equipment type, service area, and pricing
4. Storm surge pricing transparency: see current demand and estimated wait times before booking
5. Seasonal contracts: book a plower for the entire winter with guaranteed response times
6. Photo verification: before/after photos of completed work for quality assurance
7. Recurring scheduling for regular snowfall areas
8. Elderly/disability priority queue with subsidized rates through municipal partnerships

## Market Size
- **TAM**: $22B — US snow removal market

## Existing Alternatives
- **Plowz & Mowz**: The closest competitor, but has limited geographic coverage and inconsistent service quality
- **TaskRabbit**: Offers snow removal as a category but plowers are not specialized
- **Local Facebook groups**: Common for finding plowers but no tracking, no guarantees, no payment processing
- **Seasonal contracts with landscapers**: Reliable but require commitment and aren't on-demand
- **DIY shoveling**: Free but dangerous, time-consuming, and physically impossible for many homeowners

## Why This Wins
- Real-time tracking is transformative for snow removal — knowing when the plower is coming eliminates the biggest frustration
- Photo verification ensures quality and prevents disputes
- Seasonal contracts provide plowers with guaranteed income and homeowners with reliable service
- Elderly priority queue creates goodwill and potential municipal partnership revenue
- Hyper-local focus in snow-belt cities means you can dominate one market before expanding
