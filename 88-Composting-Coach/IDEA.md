## The Problem
27% of US households have tried composting (National Waste & Recycling Association), but 50% of beginners quit within the first year due to problems: smelly compost (too much nitrogen), dry crumbly piles (not enough moisture), pest infestations (wrong materials added), and slow decomposition (wrong carbon-to-nitrogen ratio). The $1.8B composting market is growing 6% annually as cities ban food waste from landfills (California, Vermont, and others now mandate composting). Despite this growth, there's almost no personalized guidance — YouTube videos give generic advice that doesn't account for your specific setup type (bin, tumbler, pile, or vermicompost), climate zone, or available materials.

## The Solution
1. Setup profile: choose your composting method (bin, tumbler, pile, vermicompost), size, and climate zone
2. "Can I compost this?" quick search: type or photograph any item for instant yes/no/with-conditions guidance
3. Carbon-to-nitrogen ratio tracker: log what you add and get real-time balance recommendations
4. Turning schedule with reminders tailored to your method (tumblers need turning every 3-5 days, piles every 2-3 weeks)
5. Troubleshooting wizard: describe symptoms (smell, pests, slow decomposition) and get specific fixes
6. Moisture level guidance based on weather data in your area
7. "Ready to use" timeline: estimated completion date based on what you've added and your method
8. Seasonal tips: what changes in winter vs. summer composting for your climate

## Existing Alternatives
- **YouTube / blogs**: Abundant but generic advice, not tailored to your specific setup or climate
- **Lomi / electric composters**: Hardware solution ($400-500), not a guidance tool
- **Master Composter programs**: Local classes run by municipalities, helpful but one-time and not on-demand
- **Composting books**: Comprehensive but static — can't troubleshoot your specific problem in real-time
- **Reddit r/composting**: Active community but responses take hours and advice quality varies wildly

## Why This Wins
- Personalized guidance based on setup type, climate, and materials is the core differentiator
- "Can I compost this?" search is a sticky daily-use feature that keeps users opening the app
- Troubleshooting wizard solves the #1 reason beginners quit (smelly/pest-infested compost)
- Carbon-to-nitrogen ratio tracker gamifies the process and teaches composting science naturally
- Growing regulatory tailwinds: as more cities mandate composting, the market expands automatically
